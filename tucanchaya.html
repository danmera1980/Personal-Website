TuCanchaYa! Group Project

Henry - Final Project

It is a web platform where users and sport field owners can manage reservations for fields and courts of the different sports.

Our main objective is to provide users a simple to use platform in order for them to reserve their courts or fields in their favorite sport, and for establishments the possibility to manage these reservations.

Scheduling

The client can make a reservation for a court or field of their preferred sport, by registering into the site and making a payment using MercadoPago.

Searching

The client has the possibility to search for courts or fields by location, and sport, or can even select their previously saved favorite court.

Establishments

Establishments can register, add multiple sites, and courts, with specific details and prices per hour, their preferred schedule, and load different images and pictures for their fields. Paying a commission por each reservation made using the application.

Our System

The system manages all information that establishments need regarding their reservations and schedules, giving them a simple solution for managing their reservations. They are able to generate reports, make in-site reservations for customers who show up to the establishment or contact them by phone.
